CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR UTAH

A COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION ALIGNED TO UTAH'S GENERAL FINANCIAL LITERACY STANDARDS

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Utah's General Financial Literacy (GFL) standards. As the first state to mandate financial literacy education in 2008, Utah has established a rigorous framework of seven strands that our 45-chapter curriculum addresses with engaging, interactive content designed to exceed state requirements.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around the 7 Utah GFL Strands, with multiple chapters per strand. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Foundation

Core concepts, definitions, and theoretical understanding with interactive discussions and skill-building activities.

Day 2: Learning Lab

Practical application through hands-on activities, simulations, and real-world problem solving with interactive skill builders.
