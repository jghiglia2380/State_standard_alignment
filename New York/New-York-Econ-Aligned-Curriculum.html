CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR NEW YORK

A MODERN SOLUTION FOR NEW YORK'S ECONOMICS GRADUATION REQUIREMENT

PFL Academy's 45-chapter course offers a complete, 1:1 solution for fulfilling New York's half-credit graduation requirement in Economics, the Enterprise System, and Finance. Our curriculum uniquely blends the foundational economic principles mandated by the state with the essential, real-world application of personal finance, creating a relevant and engaging experience for today's students.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized to mirror the flow of a traditional economics course while integrating critical life skills. Each of the 45 chapters follows our proven two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Foundation

Core concepts, definitions, and theoretical understanding with interactive discussions and skill-building activities.

Day 2: Learning Lab

Practical application through hands-on activities, simulations, and real-world problem solving with interactive skill builders.
