CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR KENTUCKY

A COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION ALIGNED TO THE KENTUCKY ACADEMIC STANDARDS (KAS) FOR FINANCIAL LITERACY

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with the Kentucky Academic Standards (KAS) for Financial Literacy. Our 45-chapter curriculum addresses all eight KAS standards with engaging, interactive content designed to meet Kentucky educational requirements while providing essential career readiness support for Individual Learning Plan (ILP) initiatives.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around the 8 Kentucky Academic Standards, with multiple chapters per standard. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Content Delivery

  • Structured introduction to key financial concepts
  • Multimedia presentations and instructor-led discussions
  • Real-world examples and case studies
  • Interactive skill builder activities
  • Guided reflection and comprehension checks

Day 2: Learning Lab

  • Hands-on application of concepts through learning stations
  • Interactive digital simulations and tools
  • AI-powered assessment and feedback
  • Portfolio development activities
  • Collaborative problem-solving exercises
