COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR ALABAMA'S FINANCIAL LITERACY MANDATE

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Alabama's Career Preparedness course requirements and the new HB164 Personal Financial Literacy mandate. Our 45-chapter curriculum addresses all 23 Career Preparedness benchmarks while preparing students for Alabama's new state financial literacy assessment.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around Alabama's dual financial literacy requirements. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Content Delivery

  • Structured introduction to key financial concepts
  • Multimedia presentations and instructor-led discussions
  • Real-world examples and Alabama-specific case studies
  • Interactive skill builder activities
  • Guided reflection and comprehension checks

Day 2: Learning Lab

  • Hands-on application through career-focused learning stations
  • Interactive digital simulations and planning tools
  • AI-powered assessment and personalized feedback
  • Portfolio development for career readiness
  • Collaborative problem-solving and peer learning
