NEBRASKA ALIGNED CURRICULUM

CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR NEBRASKA

A COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION ALIGNED TO NEBRASKA'S FINANCIAL LITERACY ACT

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Nebraska's Financial Literacy Act requirements. Our 45-chapter curriculum addresses all eight statutory topic areas with engaging, interactive content designed to meet Nebraska's 5-credit course requirements.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around Nebraska's eight statutory financial literacy topics, with multiple chapters per topic area. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Content Delivery

  • Structured introduction to key financial concepts
  • Multimedia presentations and instructor-led discussions
  • Real-world examples and case studies
  • Interactive skill builder activities
  • Guided reflection and comprehension checks

Day 2: Learning Lab

  • Hands-on application of concepts through learning stations
  • Interactive digital simulations and tools
  • AI-powered assessment and feedback
  • Portfolio development activities
  • Collaborative problem-solving exercises
