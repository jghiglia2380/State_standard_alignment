GEORGIA ALIGNED CURRICULUM

1:1 CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT WITH GEORGIA SB 220 STANDARDS

COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR GEORGIA'S FINANCIAL LITERACY MANDATE

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Georgia's SB 220 Personal Financial Literacy mandate and the redesigned Personal Finance and Economics course. Our 45-chapter curriculum balances personal finance education (50%) with economics content (50%) while preparing students for the Georgia Milestones Economics assessment.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum integrates personal finance with economic principles for 11th and 12th grade students. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Integrated Content Delivery

  • Personal finance concepts with economic foundations
  • Market analysis and economic theory applications
  • Georgia-specific economic and financial case studies
  • Interactive economic modeling and financial planning
  • Economic policy impact on personal financial decisions

Day 2: Applied Economics Lab

  • Economic simulation with personal finance applications
  • Market analysis tools and financial decision modeling
  • Georgia Milestones assessment preparation
  • Economic research and financial planning projects
  • Collaborative economic problem-solving exercises
