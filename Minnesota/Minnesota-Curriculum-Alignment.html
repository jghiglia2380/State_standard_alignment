CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR MINNESOTA

A COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION ALIGNED TO MINNESOTA'S ECONOMICS STANDARDS

PFL Academy offers a complete, 45-chapter curriculum providing 1:1 alignment with the Minnesota K-12 Academic Standards in Social Studies for Economics. Our curriculum addresses all four state strands—including Personal Finance, Microeconomics, and Macroeconomics—with engaging content designed to meet all graduation requirements.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized to meet the 4 Minnesota Strands. To provide a clear and teachable structure, the comprehensive "Personal Finance" strand is organized into logical, thematic sub-standards. Each of the 45 chapters follows our two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Foundation

Core concepts, definitions, and theoretical understanding with interactive discussions and skill-building activities.

Day 2: Learning Lab

Practical application through hands-on activities, simulations, and real-world problem solving with interactive skill builders.
