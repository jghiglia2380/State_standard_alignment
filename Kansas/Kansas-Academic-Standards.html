CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT FOR KANSAS

A COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION ALIGNED TO KANSAS'S FINANCIAL LITERACY STANDARDS

PFL Academy's comprehensive curriculum provides complete alignment with the Kansas Curricular Standards for Financial Literacy. Our curriculum addresses all five Kansas standards with engaging, interactive content designed to meet all state educational requirements for high school graduation.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum is organized around the 5 Kansas Standards, with multiple chapters per standard. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Foundation

Core concepts, definitions, and theoretical understanding with interactive discussions and skill-building activities.

Day 2: Learning Lab

Practical application through hands-on activities, simulations, and real-world problem solving with interactive skill builders.
