COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR MISSISSIPPI'S COLLEGE AND CAREER READINESS MANDATE

PFL Academy's comprehensive curriculum provides complete alignment with Mississippi's College and Career Readiness (CCR) graduation requirement. Our 45-chapter curriculum integrates financial literacy education with college and career preparation, meeting Mississippi's mandate for comprehensive post-secondary readiness while incorporating 2025 AI and technology updates.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum integrates financial literacy with college and career readiness for comprehensive post-secondary preparation. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Integrated Foundation Building

  • Financial literacy concepts with career readiness integration
  • College planning and financial preparation strategies
  • Mississippi-specific economic and career case studies
  • Interactive planning tools and career exploration
  • Technology and AI integration for modern workforce preparation

Day 2: Applied Readiness Lab

  • Hands-on financial and career planning applications
  • Digital portfolio development and career preparation
  • AI-powered assessment and personalized feedback
  • College and career transition planning projects
  • Collaborative problem-solving and peer mentoring
