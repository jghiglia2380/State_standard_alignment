1:1 CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT WITH TENNESSEE PERSONAL FINANCE STANDARDS

COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR TENNESSEE'S FINANCIAL LITERACY MANDATE

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Tennessee's Personal Finance graduation requirement, building on over a decade of successful implementation since 2013. Our 45-chapter curriculum covers all required topics while incorporating Tennessee-specific economic advantages and career opportunities.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum addresses Tennessee's half-credit Personal Finance requirement with comprehensive coverage of income, money management, spending and credit, saving and investing. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Financial Foundation Building

  • Core personal finance concepts with Tennessee context
  • Real-world examples from Tennessee's major industries
  • Interactive financial planning and decision-making tools
  • Tennessee-specific economic advantages and opportunities
  • Career integration and financial goal alignment

Day 2: Applied Financial Skills Lab

  • Hands-on financial planning and management applications
  • Digital financial tools and technology integration
  • Tennessee career cluster financial planning exercises
  • Portfolio development and financial goal tracking
  • Collaborative financial problem-solving and peer learning
