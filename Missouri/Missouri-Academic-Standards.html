1:1 CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT WITH MISSOURI PERSONAL FINANCE STANDARDS

COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR MISSOURI'S FINANCIAL LITERACY LEADERSHIP

PFL Academy's comprehensive financial literacy curriculum provides complete alignment with Missouri's seven Personal Finance standards, building on Missouri's "A" grade leadership in financial literacy education. Our 45-chapter curriculum addresses all requirements while supporting multiple implementation pathways including standalone courses and curriculum integration options.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum addresses Missouri's seven Personal Finance standards through comprehensive coverage and flexible implementation. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Standards-Based Foundation

  • Missouri Personal Finance standards-aligned instruction
  • Real-world Missouri economic examples and case studies
  • Interactive financial planning and decision-making tools
  • State standardized assessment preparation
  • Missouri-specific economic advantages and opportunities

Day 2: Applied Financial Skills Lab

  • Hands-on application of Missouri's seven standards
  • Missouri standardized test preparation and practice
  • Portfolio development and standards mastery demonstration
  • Pathway-specific career and financial planning exercises
  • Collaborative financial problem-solving and peer learning
