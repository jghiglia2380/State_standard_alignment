VIRGINIA ALIGNED CURRICULUM

1:1 CURRICULUM ALIGNMENT WITH VIRGINIA SOL STANDARDS

COMPREHENSIVE SOLUTION FOR VIRGINIA'S ECONOMICS & PERSONAL FINANCE MANDATE

PFL Academy's comprehensive curriculum provides complete alignment with Virginia's full-credit Economics and Personal Finance graduation requirement. Our 45-chapter curriculum addresses all 18 Standards of Learning (SOL) with integrated economic principles and personal finance applications, preparing students for Virginia's comprehensive assessment system.

CURRICULUM STRUCTURE

The PFL Academy curriculum integrates economics and personal finance through Virginia's 18 SOL standards. Each chapter follows a two-day instructional model:

Day 1: Integrated Economics & Finance

  • Economic principles with personal finance applications
  • Market analysis and consumer decision-making frameworks
  • Virginia-specific economic development and opportunities
  • SOL-aligned interactive learning and economic modeling
  • Policy analysis and personal financial impact assessment

Day 2: Applied Economics Lab

  • Economic simulation with personal finance decision making
  • Market research and financial planning applications
  • Virginia SOL assessment preparation and practice
  • W!SE certification preparation and career readiness
  • Economic policy analysis and personal planning projects
